Hi, my name is Nick Hemingway but by friends thought that was a mouthful so they started calling me “Nem”. I am a 23 year old developer lucky enough to be living in the beautiful city of Kelowna, BC. I graduated university in 2021 and have been working as a developer for Twin Creek Media ever since. During my time there I have gained plenty experience developing websites/web applications and have been given many opportunities to even spearhead entire projects on my own.

Development was a natural fit for me that I sort of came into by accident. Initially I started my university degree wanting to pursue photography and videography but one programming class was all it took to change my mind. Outside of work I enjoy spending my time outside doing activities like camping, hiking and dirtbiking. I am a strong believer in work life balance!
